Changelog

Stay up-to-date with the latest updates, improvements, and new features of Spurvo!

Welcome to the Spurvo Changelog—your source for all the latest updates, improvements, and new features of our platform. Here, we keep you informed on everything that’s changing at Spurvo, so you can make the most of our evolving features.

Each update is aimed at improving your experience with our platform, whether it's new ways to capture user feedback, enhanced analytics, or better customization options for your roadmaps. Our goal is to make product development more intuitive, engaging, and efficient for you and your team.

Check this page regularly for detailed release notes, and don’t miss out on new features that could help you build better products, faster. Stay tuned for our upcoming feature rollouts that will keep you ahead in the feedback game.
